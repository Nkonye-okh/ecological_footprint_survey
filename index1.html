Informed Consent Form

Welcome and thank you for your interest in our Ecological survey!

Information and purpose

The study in which you are asked to participate focuses on what kind of language knowledge is measured by each test.

Participation

You are asked to participate in a 60-70 minute session two times throughout the year: once in January-February, 2019 and in September-October, 2019. You will complete

  • two tasks on the web lasting 30 minutes, and
  • in the lab, four additional tasks will be provided.

Completion of all six tasks will last 60-70 minutes.

Tasks

You will be asked to complete the following tasks:

  1. Background questionnaire
  2. A task on your eating habits
  3. A task on your travel habits
  4. A task on your home culture

Compensation

You will receive 30 Euros for completing all tasks: 10 dollars immediately after completing two language use logs and 20 dollars immediately after completing ALL TWO sessions.

Benefits and Risks

By participating in this research, you will be able to understand how different test types demand different kinds of language processing. Also, your participation will contribute to the field of second language acquisition, which investigates how language is processed and acquired. There are no known risks associated with participating in this study .

Your rights to participate, say no, or withdraw

Participation in this study is completely voluntary; you may withdraw from the study at any time and with no penalty. If you don’t want your test used for the study, your decision will not affect your grades, your classes, or anything else at Tübingen University or any other places. You can also decide not to answer any particular question without any problem. By completing this lab task, you are voluntarily agreeing to participate in this study.

Confidentiality

All information collected is completely confidential. Your specific name and identifying information will not be associated with any part of the written report of the research. Audio files and test results will not be shared with anyone but the PI.
